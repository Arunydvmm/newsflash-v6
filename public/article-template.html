Preparation Tips for NEET UG 2026

1. Create a Structured Study Plan

Divide your preparation into phases:

  • Phase 1 (April-May): Complete syllabus coverage
  • Phase 2 (May-June): Revision and practice tests
  • Phase 3 (June 15-20): Final revision and mock tests

2. Focus on High-Weightage Topics

"Success in NEET comes from mastering the fundamentals and practicing consistently. Don't chase every topic equally; focus on high-weightage areas first."

Topics that carry more weight in NEET:

  • Genetics & Evolution (Biology)
  • Organic Chemistry (Chemistry)
  • Mechanics & Electromagnetism (Physics)

3. Practice with Mock Tests

Solve at least 2-3 full-length mock tests per week to:

  • Understand the exam pattern
  • Improve time management
  • Identify weak areas
  • Build confidence

4. Maintain Health & Consistency

  • Sleep 7-8 hours daily
  • Exercise for 30 minutes
  • Avoid stress and anxiety
  • Study consistently without burnout

Exam Day Tips

  • Reach the exam center 30 minutes early
  • Carry all required documents (Admit card, ID proof, etc.)
  • Read questions carefully before answering
  • Manage time: Allocate 1.2 minutes per question
  • Attempt easier questions first, then move to difficult ones
  • Don't leave questions blank unless necessary
